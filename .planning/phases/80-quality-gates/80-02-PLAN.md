Implement conditional video recording on E2E test failure in CI environments only.

Purpose: Video recordings provide complete reproduction context for CI failures (mouse movements, page transitions, loading states). Output: Enhanced conftest.py with CI-aware video recording, artifacts/videos directory, CI workflow with video upload, and tests verifying the functionality.
</objective>
